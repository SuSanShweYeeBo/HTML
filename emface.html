Emface The Treatment for Facial Skin and Muscles

EMFACE, approved for its effectiveness and safety by US FDA and worldwide, is the world’s first device to simultaneously lift and stimulate facial muscles and skin and reduce fine lines and fat.

What is EMFACE and how does it work?

EMFACE, developed by BTL, applies a patented combination of Synchronized Radiofrequency and the HIFES facial muscle stimulation. While HIFES restores and elevates support of facial tissue to create the lifting effect, radiofrequency (RF), heating the dermis, damage fat cells and encourages collagen and elastin production simultaneously.
