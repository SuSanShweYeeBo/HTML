Vaser Liposuction at Luxe Aesthetics Clinic

Gone are the days when liposuction was synonymous with discomfort and lengthy recovery. At Luxe Aesthetics in Bangkok, we use the latest breakthrough in cosmetic surgery – VASER Liposuction. Originating from the USA, this innovative technique has revolutionized fat removal and body contouring, ensuring safety, minimal discomfort, and effective results.

What is VASER Liposuction?

VASER Liposuction sets itself apart from traditional liposuction techniques through its use of innovative ultrasound technology. This method, known as Vibration Amplification of Sound Energy at Resonance (VASER), selectively targets fat cells while preserving surrounding tissues.
The core of VASER Liposuction technology revolves around its ultrasound capability. The ultrasound waves are tuned to specifically target fat cells, causing them to emulsify or liquefy. This liquefaction of fat cells is a crucial aspect of VASER technology, as it enables the removal of fat more efficiently and with less force than traditional liposuction methods. The use of ultrasound also contributes to less bruising and trauma to the body, facilitating a quicker and smoother recovery process.
