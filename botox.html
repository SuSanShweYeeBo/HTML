Botox at Luxe Aesthetics Clinic

As time goes by, we all start to see the telltale signs of aging on our faces—wrinkles and fine lines begin to appear, especially in areas of frequent expression like the forehead, around the eyes, and between the eyebrows.
At Luxe Aesthetics, our Botox treatments are tailored to address these very concerns, offering a sought-after solution to refresh and refine your appearance, gently turning back the clock on visible aging.

What Is Botox Made Of, and How Does It Work?

Botox is made from an ingredient called Botulinum Toxin Type A. When it’s injected into muscles on your face, it helps relax them by temporarily stopping them from receiving signals to contract or tighten. As a result, the skin above these muscles becomes noticeably smoother, reducing the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines.
The great thing about Botox is that it works only where it’s injected, so the rest of your face moves naturally and keeps its normal expressions.
