J-Plasma: Quick & Safe Skin Tightening with Minimal Downtime

J-Plasma is an advanced technology from the United States that combines cool helium plasma with radiofrequency energy to effectively tighten skin.
This minimally invasive procedure provides a significant advantage in rejuvenating and firming sagging skin, with reduced downtime and minimal scarring compared to traditional surgical methods. Both the United States and Thailand’s FDA have approved J-Plasma for its safety and effectiveness.

How J-plasma works

The procedure involves inserting a small tube under the skin, which delivers cool helium plasma, also known as cool inert gas, along with radio frequency (RF) energy. This creates a plasma flow at the targeted area, applying heat at 85 degrees Celsius to effectively contract and tighten skin and tissue fibers.
